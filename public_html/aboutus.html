About Us

Impact Volution well establish as a management solution firm, we exist throughout prime area in Malaysia such as Kuala Lumpur, Klang Valley, Johor Bahru, Melaka and Penang. We provide leading learning solution coupled with our unique approach in delivering impactful learning experience. We focus on your Employee, operation and business system development, as we believe this three will be the core in today business world.

Our team of experienced and qualified Consultant/Facilitator is not only committed in delivering values and impact in the workshop, but also to follow through the growth of your people and organization to ensure sustainable growth. In our approach we practised global recognize tools and assessment to enhance our learning experience. With this we able to track and custom made our approach to almost every individual for a better tomorrow.

Experiential learning or better known as “ learning through reflection on doing “ is developed by David A. Klob a famous American Educational Theorist stated that effective learning start from enabling participants to have concrete experience on the topic of learning which will bring a Aha moment or moment of sudden realization within the participants. Next follow by facilitation of reflecting and conceptualise the learning through series of brain storming and tools introduction.

The last stage will be Experiment on the concept that developed in the workshop. Impact Volution strongly apply this concept in our services which started from customized able experience of workshop, follow by facilitation by our experienced consultant, follow up after the workshop with the participants and the person that oversee the training, for a hand to hand growth experience with our clientele. We also used globally recognise Psychometric tools to support our clientele growth
